Questions, answered plainly

Clear answers. No jargon.

What exactly do you build?

Three things, usually in combination: AI voice and chat agents that handle inbound enquiries, back-office automations that remove manual work, and web pages built to capture and qualify leads. Everything connects into the tools you already use.

Do I need to replace my existing software?

No. We build around your current CRM, calendar, and tools. The goal is to make what you already have work harder, not to start you on a migration project.

How long does implementation take?

Most single systems go live in about two weeks from the day scope is approved. Larger, multi-system builds are staged so you see value at each step rather than waiting for one big launch.

What if the AI gives a customer wrong information?

Agents are constrained to your approved knowledge base and scripts, with guardrails and fallback-to-human handoff for anything outside scope. We test extensively before launch and monitor transcripts after, so issues are caught and corrected quickly.

How do you handle our data and our customers' data?

Your data stays in your accounts, under your ownership. We use least-privilege access, sign an NDA on request, and hand over full control at the end of every build. We never resell or train on your data.

How do you charge?

A fixed project price agreed in writing before work starts, based on the scope from your audit. Ongoing maintenance, if you want it, is a separate flat monthly amount. No hourly surprises.
