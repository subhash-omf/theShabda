Plain answers, no jargon

Questions, answered.

Is it a real voice agent, or just a chatbot?
It's a real AI voice agent — it picks up the phone, listens, and talks back in a natural voice, qualifying the buyer on the call. WhatsApp is the follow-up channel after the call, not the main event. Want to hear it? Call us and the agent will pick up.
What's the difference between Plan 1 and Plan 2?
Plan 1 (Qualify) is the AI voice agent that answers and qualifies every call, then follows up on WhatsApp so no lead goes cold. Plan 2 (Qualify + Grow) adds the Meta Ads loop: the qualification signals from those calls feed back into your ad targeting, so your spend chases real buyers, not clicks. Plan 2 includes everything in Plan 1.
How does the Meta Ads optimisation actually work?
When the voice agent qualifies a caller — the right budget, BHK and intent — that outcome is sent back to Meta as a conversion signal, and the qualified buyers seed look-alike audiences. Meta then spends more of your budget on people like your real buyers. You see cost per qualified lead, not just cost per click, in a weekly report. We work within your own ad account.
Is it really trained on real estate?
Yes. The voice agent is tuned on how real estate buyers actually call — BHK, carpet area, budget, possession, locality, loan questions — and on how strong sales teams qualify, book site visits and follow up. We then retrain on your projects, inventory and tone before launch. The same engine also runs for clinics & dental, local services and D2C.
What languages can it speak?
English plus major Indian languages — Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, Kannada, Marathi and more — switching to whatever the caller speaks. We tune the script and accent for your audience before launch.
Will I have to switch my CRM or tools?
No. We build around your current stack — your phone line, WhatsApp, CRM, calendar, Meta and n8n / Make / Zapier. The aim is to make what you already have respond faster, not to start a migration.
Where does our data live?
In your own accounts, owned by you. We work on least-privilege access, sign an NDA on request, and never resell your data or train on it.
